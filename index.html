A patient brings only their symptoms. 病人只带来症状。

From the first complaint to the final treatment — every step in between requires clinical reasoning, not memorisation. 从第一句主诉开始，到最终的治疗方案——这中间每一步，都需要临床推理，而不是知识堆砌。

School education lays a foundation nothing can replace — channels, points, herbs, and pattern theory all come from there. But between knowing and treating lies one more step: organising that knowledge into a chain of reasoning. And that step is the one least often trained. 院校教育打下的基础无可替代——经络、腧穴、方药、辨证的知识体系都来自那里。但从掌握知识到看好病，中间还隔着一步：把知识组织成一条推理链。而这一步，恰恰最少被专门训练。

The TCM Clinical Reasoning System starts where every patient encounter starts — the chief complaint. Like a detective working a case, it guides you step by step: 中医临床推理体系从主诉出发，以破案的方式逐步推进：

Symptom → Pathomechanism → Pattern → Treatment Principle → Prescription 症状 → 病机 → 辨证 → 治法 → 处方

Every step has its logic. Every step can be learned. 每一步都有逻辑，每一步都可以被学会。

This reasoning — with Qi ascent–descent and exit–entry as its master frame — runs through the entire platform: the theory and points modules set the coordinates and the foundations; the formulas, internal medicine, and pain modules are the same reasoning at work in each clinical field. 这套推理以升降出入为纲，贯穿全站：理论与经穴模块讲清坐标与根基；方药、内科、疼痛各模块，是同一套推理在具体病域的落地。
