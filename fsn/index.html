🏛️ 历史渊源与经典根基 / Historical Roots ▼

浮针于1996年由符仲华在南京行医时发明：将针压贴皮肤表面有时能止痛；极浅斜刺后配合横向扫散，效果显著。此后历经四个发展阶段：

FSN invented June 1996 by Fu Zhonghua in Nanjing through clinical serendipity — pressing needle against skin surface relieved pain; extremely shallow insertion with lateral sweeping produced excellent results. Four development stages:

  1. 1996–1998 初期：仅用于急性肌肉软组织疼痛Initial: only acute musculoskeletal soft tissue pain
  2. 1998–2005 成熟：专用软套管针具问世，系统教学，首批发表Maturation: soft-tube cannula needle developed, systematic teaching, first publications
  3. 2005–2015 扩展：再灌注手法确立为核心步骤；原发/继发部位理论形成；国际教学开始Expansion: reperfusion approach established as core step; primary/secondary scene framework; international teaching begins
  4. 2015–至今 / present 精化：前/后肌源性理论；现代结缔组织生物学整合；循证证据快速积累Refinement: pre/post-muscular theory; modern connective tissue biology integration; rapid evidence accumulation
《灵枢》经典对应Classical Lingshu Parallels 「毛刺」——浅刺皮部治浅表痹症；「浮刺」（旁入而浮之，以治肌急而寒者）——从病变侧方进针治寒性肌痉挛，与浮针进针方式几乎完全吻合。 Maoci (superficial skin needling for shallow Bi syndrome); Fuci ("inserting obliquely from the side, floating, to treat cold muscle spasm") — the Fuci technique almost exactly describes FSN's insertion approach.

🏗️ 皮下层：为何选择这里？/ The Subcutaneous Layer: Why Here? ▼

皮下层（浅筋膜层）位于真皮之下、深筋膜之上，由疏松网状结缔组织构成。浮针针体始终在此层内操作，不穿越深筋膜。

The subcutaneous layer (superficial fascia) lies between dermis above and deep fascia below, composed of loose areolar connective tissue. The FSN needle operates entirely within this layer and never crosses the deep fascia.

3️⃣ 远程攻击 / Remote Attack ▼

皮下疏松结缔组织是全身最大的连续结缔组织网络，具有非极性（各向同性传导）特征。在远离患肌的部位进针，机械信号同样能通过筋膜网络到达靶肌，产生等效的治疗效果。

The subcutaneous loose CT is the body's largest continuous fascial network with non-polar (isotropic) properties. Needling far from the target muscle transmits equivalent mechanical signals through the fascial network.

兔模型实验证据 (Fu et al. 2012)近端与远端皮下进针对肌筋膜触发点兴奋性（终板噪声振幅）的抑制效果完全相同，证实机械信号通过皮下筋膜网络的远程传导。/ Proximal and distal subcutaneous needling produced identical MTrP suppression, confirming remote mechanical signal transmission.
临床应用：一针多肌 / One Needle, Multiple Muscles当多块肌肉在同一区域紧张时，可选择一个远端进针点覆盖整个肌群，减少穿刺次数。When multiple muscles in a region are tightened, a single remote entry point can cover the entire group, reducing needle insertions.
4️⃣ 神经修复机制 / Neural Repair Mechanism ▼

浮针的治疗效应不限于筋膜-机械模型，还包括直接的神经修复过程——为神经病理性疼痛（如幻肢痛）的疗效提供科学基础。

FSN's effects extend beyond the fascial-mechanical model to include direct neurological repair — providing scientific basis for effectiveness in neuropathic pain including phantom limb pain.

病理基础：患肌理论
Pathology: The Tightened Muscle — The Only Target

浮针最核心的临床原则 / Core Clinical Principle 绝不针刺继发部位。患者的疼痛是真实的，位置是他们感受到的地方——但疼痛的原因在别处。找到患肌，在那里治疗。 Never needle the secondary scene. The patient's pain is real and located where they feel it — but the cause is elsewhere. Find the tightened muscle and treat there.
🔑 患肌定义与触诊特征 / Definition & Palpation ▼

患肌（Tightened Muscle）：肌肉在非自主激活、身体静息状态下存在的持续异常收缩——一种不需要结构破坏的功能性病变。

Tightened Muscle: A sustained state of abnormal contraction at rest without voluntary activation — a functional lesion requiring no structural destruction.

触诊阳性特征 / Palpation Positive Findings

  • 肌肉硬度增加（与对侧或周围组织相比）Increased muscle firmness (compared to contralateral or surrounding tissue)
  • 可触及紧张带（taut band）或结节样区域Palpable taut band or nodular area
  • 持续按压可复制或加剧患者的熟悉症状（即使症状在远处）Sustained pressure reproduces or intensifies patient's familiar symptoms (even when distant)
  • 对应西方疼痛科学中的肌筋膜触发点（MTrP）；浮针操作于肌腹层面，无需精确定位触发点Corresponds to myofascial trigger points (MTrP) in Western pain science; FSN works at muscle belly level — no need for precise trigger point localization
剪切波弹性成像（SWE）证实：经浮针治疗后上斜方肌剪切波弹性模量显著下降，与NDI和VAS评分强相关——验证了触诊评估的临床有效性。(Chen et al. Front Neurosci. 2025) / Shear wave elastography confirmed FSN significantly reduces upper trapezius elastic modulus, strongly correlated with NDI and VAS — validating palpation-based assessment.

🎯 原发部位 vs 继发部位 / Primary Scene vs Secondary Scene ▼

✅ 原发部位（Primary Scene）

产生症状的患肌所在位置。通常与症状部位相距甚远。是浮针治疗的唯一进针目标。

Location of the tightened muscle causing symptoms. Usually far from the symptom location. The only target for FSN needling.

❌ 继发部位（Secondary Scene）

患者感受到症状的位置。症状的结果，不是原因。绝不在此进针。

Where the patient experiences symptoms. The effect, not the cause. Never needle here.

五步触诊法 / Five-Step Palpation Protocol

  1. 确定继发部位Identify the secondary scene — 患者主诉的症状精确位置exact location of patient's reported symptoms
  2. 列出原发肌候选List primary scene candidates — 根据解剖知识和牵涉痛模式推理reason from anatomy and referred pain patterns
  3. 在患者体表定位Locate each on the patient — 确认解剖位置confirm anatomical positions
  4. 肌肉完全放松状态下触诊Palpate with muscle fully relaxed — 用指腹而非指尖；感受质地而非制造疼痛；双侧对比use finger pulp not tip; feel texture not produce pain; bilateral comparison essential
  5. 评级并确定治疗优先顺序Grade and prioritize — 最紧张者优先处理most tightened muscle treated first

Needle & Technique — Step-by-Step Procedure

🔧 浮针针具结构 / FSN Needle Structure ▼
  1. 实心钢针芯Solid steel inner needle — 52mm长 · 0.6mm直径 · 斜面针尖 | 52mm length · 0.6mm diameter · beveled tip
    提供刺入皮肤所需的刚性Provides rigidity for skin puncture
  2. 软套管（关键部件）Soft-tube cannula (key component) — 49mm长 · 外径1.05mm · 柔性高分子材料 | 49mm length · 1.05mm OD · flexible polymer
    留在皮下进行扫散的工作部件——扫散时是软套管在移动，针芯尖端已退入套管内Remains in tissue for sweeping — the blunt cannula sweeps, the sharp needle tip is recessed inside it
  3. 针座Needle handle/base — 连接针芯与套管，便于操作控制Connects inner needle and cannula; provides grip
⚠️ 最容易误解的技术步骤 / Most Misunderstood Step 针芯回缩不是移除——针芯仍留在套管内，只是向后退3-5mm使锐利针尖缩入套管开口内部。此后扫散由钝性软套管在疏松结缔组织中移动，针尖始终被保护在套管内无法接触组织。这正是扫散无痛、不损伤组织的设计原理。 Partial retraction does NOT remove the inner needle — it remains inside the cannula, moved backward only 3-5mm so the sharp tip retreats INTO the cannula opening. Sweeping is then performed by the blunt soft cannula moving through loose CT; the sharp tip is protected inside throughout. This is the design principle making FSN sweeping painless and atraumatic.
📋 完整操作流程（逐步详解）/ Complete Procedure Step by Step ▼
  1. 患者体位Patient positioning — 患肌完全放松并可触及；进针点皮下层可及；患者可从当前体位执行再灌注动作。俯卧：背部/后颈；仰卧：腹部/下肢前侧；侧卧：髋侧/腰方肌Primary scene muscle fully relaxed and accessible; subcutaneous layer at insertion site accessible; patient can perform reperfusion movements. Prone: back/posterior neck; Supine: abdomen/anterior limb; Lateral: hip/QL
  2. 确定进针点Select insertion site — 进针点不是患肌本身，而是从该点可以用扫散轨迹覆盖目标肌肉的皮下层位置。通常在患肌近端或相邻，针尖指向患肌。避开感染、瘢痕、静脉曲张区域。The insertion site is NOT the primary scene muscle itself — it is a subcutaneous point from which the needle can reach the target with its sweeping trajectory. Usually proximal to or adjacent to the muscle, needle directed toward it.
  3. 皮肤消毒Skin preparation — 70%异丙醇消毒进针点，待干后进针。佩戴清洁手套。70% isopropyl alcohol or equivalent; allow to dry before inserting. Wear clean gloves.
  4. 进针（15-25°浅角）Needle insertion (15-25° shallow angle) — 针座置于拇指与前两指之间，斜面朝上，15-25°浅角进针。角度过大将穿越深筋膜。进入皮肤后稍降低角度，沿皮下层向患肌推进。感觉顺滑无阻力；皮肤随针走行处微隆（帐篷征）确认在正确层次。如有酸胀或阻力，退针调浅角度重新进针。Hold needle between thumb and first two fingers, bevel up, at 15-25° angle. Steeper angle risks crossing deep fascia. After skin penetration, reduce angle slightly and advance subcutaneously. Smooth, free, low-resistance feel + skin tenting = correct layer. Aching or resistance → too deep; withdraw and redirect shallower.
  5. 针芯回缩（3-5mm）Partial retraction of inner needle (3-5mm) — 非优势手固定套管针座；优势手将针芯手柄向后滑动约3-5mm。针芯仍在套管内——仅使锐利针尖退至套管开口内部。目视确认：针尖不再从套管末端伸出。Non-dominant hand holds cannula base; dominant hand slides inner needle handle backward ~3-5mm. Inner needle remains inside cannula — only sharp tip retreats into cannula opening. Visual confirm: tip no longer visible protruding beyond cannula end.
  6. 扫散运动Sweeping movement — 以针座为轴心，在皮下层内进行左右横向扫弧运动。每侧约30-45°。速度：约每分钟20-30个完整来回弧。患者应几乎感觉不到扫散过程。如患者感到酸胀或抽搐，说明进针过深。持续约2分钟。With needle hub as pivot, perform lateral oscillating arc — smooth, even, rhythmic. Arc ~30-45° each side. Pace: ~20-30 full arcs per minute. Patient should feel virtually nothing. Aching or twitching = needle too deep. Duration: ~2 minutes.
  7. 再灌注活动（扫散后立即进行，不拔针）Reperfusion movement (immediately after sweeping, needle in place) — 执行特异性针对患肌的主动或被动运动，每次维持抵抗10-15秒，重复2-3次，慢速控制（次数过多易造成治疗性损伤）。Perform specific active or passive movement targeting the primary scene muscle, 10-15 reps/set, slow and controlled.
  8. 即时评估Immediate reassessment — 再灌注后：触诊患肌紧张度变化；患者执行功能评估动作。改善确认治疗方向正确；无改善提示原发肌识别可能有误。After reperfusion: palpate tension change; patient performs functional assessment movement. Improvement confirms correct primary scene; no improvement → reassess primary scene identification.
  9. 留针（可选，20-30分钟）Needle retention (optional, 20-30 min) — 主动治疗阶段结束后留置于皮下层。留针期间不进行扫散；患者休息。After active treatment phase, needle may be retained in subcutaneous layer. No sweeping during retention; patient rests.
  10. 拔针Needle withdrawal — 沿进针角度顺滑拔出，不旋转。进针点轻压30秒；抗凝患者压迫2-3分钟。Smooth withdrawal along insertion angle, no rotation. Apply pressure 30 seconds; 2-3 minutes for anticoagulant patients.

再灌注手法
Reperfusion Approach — The Second Half of Every FSN Treatment

核心原则：再灌注不是可选项 / Reperfusion is Non-Optional 扫散创造再灌注条件，但血液需要"泵"才能进入缺血区。没有再灌注活动，能量危机循环无法被彻底打破，治疗效果短暂且不完整。 Sweeping creates conditions for reperfusion, but blood needs a pump to enter the ischemic zone. Without reperfusion movement, the energy crisis cycle cannot be truly broken — effects are transient and incomplete.
🎯 选择原则 / Selection Principle ▼
运动必须特异性地激活患肌 / Movement Must Specifically Engage the Primary Scene Muscle不激活患肌的运动无法向该肌肉提供泵血效应。患肌的主要功能动作决定再灌注动作选择。A movement that does not contract the primary scene muscle provides no pumping effect to that muscle. The primary action of the muscle determines the reperfusion movement.

✅ 主动再灌注（首选）/ Active (Preferred)

患者主动执行运动（自由或抗阻）。肌肉自身收缩产生更大的内压变化 → 泵血效果更强。适用于患者可执行所需动作时。

Patient performs movement voluntarily — freely or against manual resistance. Stronger pumping effect. Used when patient can perform the required movement.

〰️ 被动再灌注 / Passive

医者为患者移动肢体或躯干。适用于患者无法主动运动时（严重受限的冻结肩、儿童患者等）。效果弱于主动，但优于无再灌注。

Clinician moves patient's limb through range. Used when patient cannot perform active movement. Less powerful than active but better than none.

临床评估与诊断
Assessment & Diagnosis — Finding the Tightened Muscle

📋 问诊框架 / History-Taking Framework ▼
  1. 继发部位定位Secondary scene location — 精确描述症状位置（不仅是"膝盖痛"——前、内、外、后？哪侧？运动时还是静息时？）Precise symptom location (not just "knee pain" — anterior, medial, lateral, posterior? Which side? Motion or rest?)
  2. 症状性质Symptom character — 疼痛/僵硬/麻木/无力/活动受限/功能性症状Pain/stiffness/numbness/weakness/restricted motion/functional symptoms
  3. 加重/缓解因素Aggravating/relieving factors — 体位、运动、久坐、晨起（提示患肌群）Position, movement, prolonged sitting, morning stiffness (suggests tightened muscle group)
  4. 既往治疗史（重要诊断信息！）Prior treatment history (diagnostic information!)
    • 有力量改善但无症状缓解的物理治疗 → 肌肉是紧张而非无力
    • Physiotherapy with strength improvement but no symptom relief → muscle is tightened not simply weak
    • 糖皮质激素局部注射短期有效但不持久 → 功能性（肌筋膜）而非结构性
    • Cortisone injection temporary but not lasting relief → functional (myofascial) not structural
    • 脊柱手术后下肢症状持续 → 患肌是驱动因素，而非手术针对的结构性病变
    • Spinal surgery that did not resolve lower limb pain → tightened muscle was driving symptom, not the surgically corrected structural lesion
🖐️ 触诊五原则 / Five Palpation Principles ▼
  1. 肌肉必须完全放松Muscle must be completely relaxed — 完全支撑、卸载所有张力。患者主动保持的肌肉会整体感觉紧实，无法显示差异性病理性紧张。Fully supported, all tension unloaded. A muscle the patient is actively holding feels uniformly firm and cannot reveal differential pathological tension.
  2. 触诊是感受，不是压迫Contact is palpation, not compression — 感受组织质地，不是制造疼痛。Feel for texture, not produce pain.
  3. 用指腹，不用指尖Use finger pulp, not fingertip — 指腹接触面积更大，感受组织质地更敏感。Finger pulp has greater contact area and superior texture sensitivity.
  4. 患者症状指向继发部位Patient's report points to secondary scene — 询问"这里按压的感觉熟悉吗？"而不是"去触诊患者说痛的地方"。Ask "does this pressure feel familiar? Is this your pain?" — not "palpate where the patient says it hurts."
  5. 双侧对比是必须的Bilateral comparison is essential — 正常侧是参考基准，确认病理性紧张而非患者个体差异。The normal side is the reference baseline, confirming pathological tension vs individual variation.

治疗前转介红旗征 / Red Flags Requiring Referral First

  • Unexplained weight loss with musculoskeletal symptoms

起：冈上窝→大结节上面。功能：启动外展（0-30°）；稳定肱骨头。症状：三角肌外侧深部酸痛，有时延伸至外上髁；肩外展60-120°"痛弧"加剧。进针：颈肩交界处后上方进针指向外侧冈上窝（针在斜方肌上方皮下行走）；再灌注：肩胛面主动外展，缓慢过弧。

O: supraspinous fossa → superior facet greater tubercle. Function: initiates abduction (0-30°); stabilizes humeral head. Pattern: deep aching at lateral deltoid, sometimes to lateral epicondyle; worsened at 60-120° "painful arc". Needle: posterosuperior neck-shoulder junction toward supraspinous fossa; Reperfusion: active abduction in scapular plane, slowly through painful arc.

肩胛下肌 / Subscapularis

起：肩胛下窝→小结节及关节囊。功能：肱骨内旋；最重要的抗肱骨上移稳定力。症状：前肩深部酸痛；特征性后腕部疼痛；夜间痛、外旋受限；冻结肩外旋受限的主要原发肌。进针：臂外展90°外旋，从前腋皱襞进针指向后方肩胛下窝——腋部神经血管束邻近，进针慢、角度浅、遇锐痛/电感立即改向；再灌注：肩内旋抗阻。

O: subscapular fossa → lesser tubercle and joint capsule. Function: internal rotation; most important stabilizer against superior translation. Pattern: anterior shoulder deep aching; characteristic posterior wrist pain; night pain, restricted ER; primary scene for frozen shoulder ER loss. Needle: arm abducted 90° externally rotated, insert at anterior axillary fold toward subscapular fossa — axillary NV bundle nearby, insert slowly, shallow angle, redirect if sharp/electrical sensation; Reperfusion: internal rotation against resistance.

胸大肌 · 胸小肌 / Pectoralis Major · Minor ▼
原发肌 / Primary Scene

胸大肌 / Pectoralis Major

起：锁骨内半+胸骨+第1-6肋软骨→肱骨结节间沟外唇。症状：前胸壁痛（可误认为心脏痛）、前肩痛；牵涉至前臂内侧→尺侧两指。进针：前肩或锁骨下沿胸大肌纤维方向；再灌注：水平外展；胸前推抗阻后松开。

O: medial clavicle + sternum + ribs 1-6 → lateral lip bicipital groove. Pattern: anterior chest pain (may mimic cardiac), anterior shoulder; refers to medial forearm → ulnar two fingers. Needle: anterior shoulder or below clavicle along fibers; Reperfusion: horizontal abduction; chest press against resistance then release.

后肌源性 / Post-muscular

胸小肌 / Pectoralis Minor

最常见被忽视的后肌源性诊断。起：第3-5肋→喙突。症状：喙突区前肩/胸痛；臂丛+腋动脉在喙突下间隙受压→尺侧两指麻木（EMG正常）；限制肩上举和外旋；呼吸困难、气不够用感。进针：锁骨下胸大肌锁骨头外侧进针，指向内下方肋起源；再灌注：肩胛后缩上提；双手抱头深吸气。

Most commonly overlooked post-muscular diagnosis. O: ribs 3-5 → coracoid process. Pattern: anterior shoulder/chest at coracoid; brachial plexus + axillary artery compression → ulnar two fingers numbness (EMG normal); restricts elevation and ER; dyspnea, sense of inability to take full breath. Needle: below clavicle lateral to pect major clavicular head toward rib origin; Reperfusion: scapular retraction/elevation; deep inspiration with hands behind head.

外上髁稍远侧背侧前臂进针，沿肱桡肌腹指向远端

旋前圆肌 / Pronator Teres（假性腕管综合征）▼

🦴 腰盆部 / Lumbar & Pelvic

⭐ 腰方肌 / Quadratus Lumborum（最重要）▼
原发肌 / Primary Scene
起止点
髂腰韧带及髂嵴 → 第12肋下缘；L1-L4横突
Iliolumbar ligament and iliac crest → inferior border of 12th rib; transverse processes L1-L4
功能
腰椎同侧侧屈；同侧骨盆上提（髋上提）；用力呼气时稳定第12肋
Ipsilateral lateral flexion of lumbar spine; ipsilateral hemipelvis elevation (hip hiking); stabilizes 12th rib during forced expiration
症状
典型"腰闪了"：髂嵴和侧腹在转换动作时锐痛（上下车、翻身、起坐）；放射至前髂嵴、腹股沟、外侧大腿、大转子区（常被误诊为转子滑囊炎）
Classic "catch in the back": sharp pain at iliac crest and flank on transitional movements (car, rolling over, rising from seated); refers to anterior iliac crest, groin, outer thigh, greater trochanter (often misdiagnosed as trochanteric bursitis)
触诊⚠️
最佳体位：患侧在上侧卧位。后腋线髂嵴与第12肋之间的侧腹，向前内方按压至腰方肌（在胸腰筋膜深层）。俯卧位无法充分触诊。
Best position: lateral decubitus, affected side up. Palpate posterior axillary line between iliac crest and 12th rib, pressing anteriorly and medially to reach QL (deep to thoracolumbar fascia). Cannot be adequately palpated in prone.
进针⚠️
侧卧位。后腋线髂嵴与第12肋之间进针，指向前方腰方肌。深度控制关键——胸膜和肾邻近！
Lateral decubitus. Insert at posterior axillary line between iliac crest and 12th rib, directing anteriorly. Depth control critical — pleura and kidney nearby!
再灌注
对侧躯干侧屈；髋上提（患者节律性上提同侧骨盆）直接激活腰方肌
Contralateral lateral trunk flexion; hip hiking (rhythmically elevates ipsilateral hemipelvis) directly engages QL through its primary action
髂腰肌 · 臀小肌 · 梨状肌 / Iliopsoas · Gluteus Minimus · Piriformis ▼
髂腰肌
Iliopsoas
症状：腹股沟痛、前髋痛、腰痛（久坐加重——髂腰肌缩短；行走/上楼加重）；单侧紧张→骨盆倾斜+代偿性腰椎侧弯；行走时髋不能完全伸展提示双侧受累。进针：ASIS稍内侧进针指向后内方髂窝；弓步髋伸展（一腿向前，后膝跪地，后髋下沉）。
Pattern: groin pain, anterior hip pain, low back pain (worse with sitting — iliopsoas shortened; walking/stairs); unilateral tightness → pelvic tilt + compensatory scoliosis; inability to fully extend hip when walking = bilateral involvement. Needle: just medial to ASIS, direct posteriorly and medially along iliac fossa; lunge hip extension.
⭐ 臀小肌
Glut. Minimus
假性坐骨神经痛肌。触发点放射至后外侧大腿→小腿→踝，坐骨样分布。双侧臀小肌紧张是"双侧坐骨神经痛"最常见的原发肌——而非双侧椎间盘突出。进针：髂后上棘进针，指向前外下方大转子，沿髂骨外侧面皮下行走；再灌注：髋外展抗阻；单腿站立（对侧腿微提）。
The "pseudo-sciatica" muscle. Trigger points refer to posterior/lateral thigh→leg→ankle in sciatic distribution. Bilateral gluteus minimus tension is the most common primary scene for "bilateral sciatica" — not bilateral disc herniation. Needle: posterior iliac crest, direct anteriorly and inferiorly toward greater trochanter along outer ilium; Reperfusion: hip abduction against resistance; single-leg standing.
梨状肌
Piriformis
症状：臀深部"屁股里面"酸痛；久坐、上楼、髋内旋加重。约15%人群坐骨神经直接穿过梨状肌，其余紧邻→梨状肌紧张可压迫坐骨神经；女性可致性交痛和盆腔痛。进针：骶骨后缘进针，指向外侧大转子，皮下越过臀区；再灌注：俯卧髋内旋+外旋抗阻。
Pattern: deep buttock aching "inside the buttock"; worse with sitting, stairs, hip internal rotation. Sciatic nerve passes directly through piriformis in ~15%, alongside in remainder → compression produces sciatic symptoms; dyspareunia and pelvic pain in women. Needle: posterior sacral margin, direct laterally toward greater trochanter subcutaneously; Reperfusion: prone hip internal+external rotation against resistance.

起止点
股直肌：AIIS；股外侧肌：大转子/股外侧线；股内侧肌：股内侧线；股中间肌：股骨干前外侧面 → 经髌骨→髌腱→胫骨结节

腓肠肌
Gastrocnemius
后腿痛、夜间小腿痉挛（肌筋膜触发点现象，非电解质不足）；牵涉至腘窝和足弓；跟腱病和足底筋膜炎的重要原发肌。进针：腘窝下方近端小腿进针指向跟腱交界处；再灌注：提踵；踝背屈抗阻。
Posterior leg pain, nocturnal calf cramping (trigger point phenomenon — not electrolyte deficiency); refers to popliteal fossa and instep; significant primary for Achilles tendinopathy and plantar fasciitis. Needle: proximal calf below popliteal crease toward Achilles junction; Reperfusion: calf raises; ankle dorsiflexion against resistance.
比目鱼肌
Soleus
后足跟及跟腱区；重要：还牵涉至同侧骶髂区和下背部——当腰椎/骶髂治疗无效时应双侧评估比目鱼肌。进针：小腿内侧腓肠肌-比目鱼肌交界下方；屈膝90°孤立比目鱼肌；再灌注：屈膝位踝背屈；屈膝提踵。
Posterior heel and Achilles region; Important: also refers to ipsilateral sacroiliac and lower back — assess bilateral soleus when lumbar/SI treatment fails. Needle: medial calf below gastrocnemius-soleus junction; knee bent 90° to isolate; Reperfusion: ankle dorsiflexion with knee bent; knee-bent calf raises.
胫骨后肌
Tibialis Post.
后足跟及跟腱区（足底筋膜炎和跟腱病重要原发肌）；内踝痛；内侧纵弓支撑丧失→旋前相关损伤。进针：小腿近端后内侧进针指向胫骨；再灌注：踝内翻抗阻；单腿提踵+轻度旋后。
Posterior heel and Achilles region (significant primary for plantar fasciitis and Achilles tendinopathy); medial ankle pain; loss of medial arch → pronation injuries. Needle: posteromedial proximal calf toward tibia; Reperfusion: ankle inversion against resistance; single-leg calf raise with slight supination.
腓骨肌
Peroneal Group
外踝痛、外足痛、第5跖骨茎突痛——典型"慢性踝扭伤"（韧带已愈合但腓骨肌触发点未处理）。进针：腓骨中段外侧腿进针；再灌注：踝外翻抗阻；单腿不稳定面站立。
Lateral ankle pain, lateral foot pain, 5th metatarsal styloid pain — classic "chronic ankle sprain" (ligaments healed but peroneal trigger points untreated). Needle: mid-fibula lateral leg; Reperfusion: ankle eversion against resistance; single-leg unstable surface.

肱桡肌（最常见最一致）；桡侧腕短伸肌；桡侧腕长伸肌；指总伸肌
Brachioradialis (most common, most consistent); ECRB; ECRL; extensor digitorum communis
鉴别
触诊肱桡肌腹复制外上髁痛（不触外上髁）→ 证明肘不是痛源
Palpate brachioradialis belly reproducing epicondylar pain without touching epicondyle → proves elbow is not the source
预后
极佳。病史＜1年者多数3-4次治愈。FSN优于TENS（即刻止痛、PPT、无痛握力、PRTEE）
Excellent. Most <1yr duration resolve in 3-4 sessions. FSN superior to TENS for immediate pain relief, PPT, pain-free grip, PRTEE

慢性腰痛 / Chronic Low Back Pain Meta 17 RCTs n=1467

原发肌
腰段竖脊肌（几乎必累及，即使单侧痛也常双侧）；腰方肌；髂腰肌；臀中肌/臀小肌；梨状肌
Lumbar erector spinae (almost always, often bilateral even with unilateral pain); QL; iliopsoas; gluteus medius/minimus; piriformis
重要
"双侧坐骨神经痛"几乎从不代表双侧椎间盘突出——双侧臀小肌紧张是最常见原发肌

证据
VAS MD −1.12 / ODI MD −6.75 / JOA MD +4.52 / OR 2.77 (P<0.00001)

膝骨关节炎 / Knee Osteoarthritis Meta 30 RCTs n=2169

原发肌
股四头肌（最充分研究）；股外侧肌（外侧髌骨轨迹力）；股内侧肌（VMO失效）；股直肌；阔筋膜张肌/IT束
Quadriceps (most studied); vastus lateralis (lateral patellar tracking); vastus medialis (VMO failure); rectus femoris; TFL/IT band

Most evidence-supported FSN indication. FSN significantly superior to controls (pain, function, tissue hardness, walking speed)

足底筋膜炎 / Plantar Fasciitis

原则
足底筋膜是继发部位；小腿肌肉是原发部位。治疗小腿，而非足跟。Plantar fascia is the secondary scene; calf muscles are the primary scene. Treat the calf, not the heel.
原发肌
腓肠肌（最常见——限制背屈）；比目鱼肌（足跟及跟腱放射）；胫骨后肌（内侧足跟和足弓痛）；足底内在肌
Gastrocnemius (most common — limits dorsiflexion); soleus (heel and Achilles referral); tibialis posterior (medial heel and arch); intrinsic foot muscles
预后
3-5次多数病例。影像学跟骨骨刺通常不是痛源。
3-5 sessions for most cases. Calcaneal spurs on imaging are usually not the pain source.

慢性踝扭伤后疼痛 / Chronic Ankle Pain After Lateral Sprain

原则
韧带通常已愈合。持续疼痛源于腓骨肌触发点（继发于扭伤的肌筋膜应激），产生与韧带压痛临床无法区分的外踝痛。
Ligaments are usually healed. Persistent pain from peroneal muscle trigger points (myofascial stress secondary to the sprain), producing lateral ankle pain clinically indistinguishable from ligament tenderness.
原发肌
腓骨长短肌（首选）；胫骨前肌（若有背屈痛）；腓肠肌/比目鱼肌（若有跟腱/足跟成分）
Fibularis longus and brevis (primary); tibialis anterior (if dorsiflexion pain present); gastrocnemius/soleus (if Achilles/heel component)

手术后疼痛 / Postoperative Pain RCT n=51

证据
脊柱退行性疾病手术后：FSN组在术后1、24、48和72小时疼痛强度（BPI-T评分）显著低于假浮针组。L3椎旁肌、臀大肌和背阔肌硬度减少。无严重不良事件。
Degenerative spinal surgery patients: FSN group had significantly lower pain intensity (BPI-T scores) at 1, 24, 48, and 72 hours vs sham FSN. Reduced muscle hardness at L3 paraspinal, gluteus maximus, and latissimus dorsi. No serious adverse events.

🔶 前肌源性疾病 / Pre-Muscular Disease

冻结肩 / Frozen Shoulder (Adhesive Capsulitis) 前肌源性

分类
前肌源性。原发驱动：自身免疫/炎症性关节囊病变。肌肉紧张是疼痛和活动受限的主要来源——浮针处理肌肉成分。
Pre-muscular. Primary driver: autoimmune/inflammatory capsular disease. Muscle tension is major source of pain and restriction — FSN addresses the muscular component.
原发肌
冈下肌、肩胛下肌（几乎必累及）；胸小肌（限制上举和外旋）；前三角肌；小圆肌
Infraspinatus, subscapularis (almost universally); pectoralis minor (restricts elevation and ER); anterior deltoid; teres minor
预后
凝冻期：疼痛缓解可靠；冻结期：每次通常可增加活动度20-40°。不能消除关节囊病变，但显著加速自然病程。
Freezing phase: pain relief reliable; Frozen phase: 20-40° ROM improvement per session typically achievable. Cannot eliminate capsular process, but significantly accelerates natural history.

帕金森病 / Parkinson's Disease 前肌源性

原则
神经源性肌强直为原发驱动。FSN暂时减轻肌强直，改善活动度和疼痛。神经学驱动因素持续存在——需维持治疗。最佳治疗时机：多巴胺能药物"开"期。
Neurogenic rigidity is primary driver. FSN temporarily reduces rigidity, improves mobility and pain. Neurological driver persists — maintenance treatment required. Best scheduled during medication "on" time.
原发肌
竖脊肌（屈曲姿态）；腘绳肌（膝屈曲）；髂腰肌（髋屈曲）；胸大肌（前胸和肩部受限）；前臂屈肌
Erector spinae (flexed posture); hamstrings (knee flexion); iliopsoas (hip flexion); pectorals (anterior chest/shoulder restriction); forearm flexors

强直性脊柱炎 / Ankylosing Spondylitis 前肌源性

原则
FSN治疗反应性肌紧张成分，减轻疼痛和改善活动度；不影响基础炎症过程和强直进程。稳定期可获显著活动度改善，需维持治疗。
FSN treats reactive muscle tension component, reducing pain and improving mobility; does not affect the underlying inflammatory process or ankylosis. Significant mobility improvement achievable in stable phases, with maintenance treatment.

🟢 后肌源性疾病 / Post-Muscular Disease

尿频尿急（膀胱过度活动/非细菌性前列腺炎）/ Urinary Frequency & Urgency 后肌源性

机制
下腹肌和盆底肌紧张→压迫膀胱→改变充盈容量和尿急感阈值。尿常规正常、PSA正常。
Lower abdominal and pelvic floor muscle tension → compresses bladder → alters filling capacity and urgency threshold. Normal urinalysis, normal PSA.
原发肌
下腹直肌和腹斜肌；髂腰肌；盆底肌（肛提肌群）
Lower rectus abdominis and obliques; iliopsoas; pelvic floor (levator ani group)

原发性痛经 / Primary Dysmenorrhea 后肌源性 RCT (perineal pain)

机制
盆底、下腹肌和髂腰肌紧张→压迫子宫→减少子宫动脉血流→加剧月经性收缩疼痛。子宫本身结构正常。建议在月经前2-3天开始治疗。
Pelvic floor, lower abdominal muscles and iliopsoas tightening → compresses uterus → reduces uterine artery blood flow → intensifies menstrual contraction pain. Uterus itself structurally normal. Begin treatment 2-3 days before expected menstruation.
原发肌
下腹直肌和腹斜肌；双侧髂腰肌；盆底（肛提肌）；内收肌群
Lower rectus and obliques; bilateral iliopsoas; pelvic floor (levator ani); adductor group
预后
多数原发性痛经患者在2-3个月经周期治疗后疼痛强度和持续时间显著减轻
Most primary dysmenorrhea patients experience significant reduction in pain severity and duration within 2-3 treatment cycles

非心源性胸闷/心悸 / Non-Cardiac Chest Tightness & Palpitation 后肌源性

机制
胸壁肌紧张→压迫胸廓→减少呼吸容量→"气不够用"感和"心跳感"。心电图正常、心肌标志物正常。必须先排除结构性心脏病。
Chest wall muscle tension → compresses thorax → reduces respiratory excursion → subjective tightness and palpitation. ECG normal, cardiac biomarkers normal. Must exclude structural cardiac pathology first.
原发肌
胸大肌；胸小肌（呼吸困难）；肋间肌；前锯肌；菱形肌和胸段竖脊肌（后方限制）
Pectoralis major; pectoralis minor (dyspnea); intercostals; serratus anterior; rhomboids and thoracic erector spinae (posterior restriction)

颈部肌肉紧张相关失眠 / Insomnia Related to Cervical Muscle Tension

机制
颈部和上胸部肌紧张→持续低水平伤害性输入→阻止睡眠起始和维持；自主神经效应（交感神经张力升高）进一步损害睡眠结构。RCT副结局：FSN改善慢性颈痛患者睡眠质量。

原发肌
上斜方肌（双侧）；枕下肌群；SCM；头半棘肌；胸段竖脊肌（T1-T4）
Bilateral upper trapezius; suboccipitals; SCM; semispinalis capitis; upper thoracic erector spinae (T1-T4)
提示
首次FSN治疗后患者常报告"多年来最好的睡眠"——强有力的原发肌识别确认。
After first FSN session, patient often reports "best night's sleep in months" — strong confirmation of correct primary scene identification.

幻肢痛 / Phantom Limb Pain Case Report + SR

机制
治疗残肢邻近肌肉紧张 + 神经修复机制（促轴突再生、减少内质网应激）
Treating tightened residual limb muscles (adjacent to amputation) + neural repair mechanisms (promotes axonal regeneration, reduces ER stress)
证据
